Sopa De Queso

Description

This dish is a very common dish made in a lot of ecuadorian households. This dish is made with simple ingredients such as cheese, noodles, and egg. This dish is often nicknamed "newlyweds soup" because anybody who has never cooked or has very little ingredients can cook this!. This recipe makes for 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 medium onion cut into thing slices
  • 1 tbsp of achote
  • 2 cups of potatoes cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1.5 cups of noodles of your choice
  • 2 cups of fresco cheese
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 4 eggs
  • salt
  • oregano

Instructions

  1. Start by boiling 4 cups of water in a medium pot.
  2. Once the water is boiling add your thinly sliced onions. Add your potatos.
  3. After about 30 minutes check to see if your potatoes are done by sticking a fork through them. If the fork goes through then they are ready.
  4. Add your noodles once the potatoes are done and set to medium heat.
  5. While your noodles are cooking, in a blender add your cheese and water. Blend till the cheese is blended completely with the water.
  6. Add the cheese mixture to your pot. Stir the pot as to ensure the cheese doesn't stick to the pot.
  7. Raise the temperature to medium high and bring to a boil once more.
  8. Once soup is boiling crack in your 4 eggs.Do not mix till eggs are semi cooked.
  9. When the egg is semi cooked, stir the pot and add salt to taste.
  10. As soon as the eggs are cooked, turn off the heat and add some chopped oregano or some dried oregano flakes.
  11. Serve with rice, or by itself.
